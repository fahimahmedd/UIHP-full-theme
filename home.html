About Us

At the heart of a rapidly evolving digital landscape, Smart Unibator stands as a beacon of innovation and entrepreneurship. The program is about building innovation culture and entrepreneurship at the early stage of career building of youth.

Our mission is to foster a robust ecosystem that aligns with the Fourth Industrial Revolution's dynamic shifts, ensuring Bangladesh remains at the forefront of global advancements.

University Innovation Hub Program  is conceived by the Bangladesh High-tech Park Authority (BHTPA)  to establish a system of vibrant innovation hubs across 10 esteemed universities. These hubs are more than just centers of learning; they are the epicenters of change.
