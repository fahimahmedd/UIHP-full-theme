What is IELS?

The Entrepreneurship Lecture Series is a set of 10 lectures organised across participating universities, focusing on various aspects of innovation and entrepreneurship. These lectures are delivered by renowned national and international innovators and founders.

Who can Join? (Eligibility)

The course is open to all university students, including those not part of the Innovation Cohort, subject to seat availability. All Innovation Cohort students are required to go through this course.

Benefit of the course

  • Comprehensive Learning Experience: Dive into the essential aspects of entrepreneurship and innovation through a curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with practical exercises. Explore topics like ideation, prototype development, opportunity identification, business modeling, and financial management, tailored to cultivate your innovative thinking and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Hands-on Application: Beyond classroom learning, you'll engage in practical exercises, case studies, and project work that simulate real-world challenges. This hands-on approach ensures you not only understand theoretical concepts but can apply them to develop innovative solutions to actual problems.
  • Access to Industry Leaders: Learn from the best in the business. The course features guest lectures from industry professionals who share their journeys, insights, and lessons learned. This exposure to successful entrepreneurs and innovators offers invaluable inspiration and networking opportunities.
  • Skill Development: Equip yourself with a versatile set of skills that are highly sought after in today's job market. Whether you're aiming to launch your startup or innovate within an existing organization, the skills you acquire will be your asset in navigating the entrepreneurial landscape.
  • Fostering an Entrepreneurial Mindset: Cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset characterized by creativity, resilience, and adaptability. This mindset will empower you to see opportunities where others see obstacles and to persevere in the face of challenges.
  • Certification of Achievement: Upon completion, you'll receive a certificate that not only serves as a testament to your commitment and skills but also enhances your resume, signaling to potential employers your readiness to contribute to innovative and entrepreneurial endeavors.
  • Gateway to Further Opportunities: Successfully completing the course opens doors to further engagement with the innovation ecosystem, including participation in accelerators, access to seed funding, and opportunities for continued mentorship and growth.

Who are the Instructors?

The Certificate Course on Innovation and Entrepreneurship is led by a distinguished group of instructors, carefully chosen for their profound knowledge and experience in the realms of innovation, business development, and entrepreneurship. These instructors are seasoned professionals and academics who bring a wealth of real-world insights, groundbreaking research findings, and entrepreneurial success stories to the classroom. They are dedicated to nurturing your innovative thinking and entrepreneurial skills, guiding you through the entire process of transforming ideas into viable business ventures. With their guidance, you will explore the intricacies of ideation, market analysis, business model development, financial planning, and pitching, all while fostering the critical and creative thinking necessary for success in today's dynamic business landscape. Engaging with these experts offers a unique opportunity to gain valuable insights and develop the competencies essential for leading change and creating impact in various sectors of the economy.
